Welcome to The Jewel Hotel – a hidden gem in the heart of Manhattan, perfect for urban adventurers.

Located steps from Rockefeller Center, Times Square, Broadway, Radio City Music Hall, and 5th Avenue shopping, The Jewel places you at the center of NYC's excitement.

After exploring, unwind in modern guest rooms with high-speed Wi-Fi and smart TVs. Enjoy complimentary cappuccinos, bottled water, and attentive service, ensuring your New York City stay is unforgettable.
